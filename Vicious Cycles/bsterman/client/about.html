~ About ~


Welcome to Vicious Cycles, where we “pair” you with your perfect match, everytime, guaranteed. We started this company a little over 37 years ago, with the expressed goal of bringing something new to our local Bicycle culture in our small town. What started as a small shop building relationships with our friends, family and neighbors blossomed into something new entirely. The vicious Cycles family has been growing larger and stronger for 37 years and counting, we hope to make you a part of it!
